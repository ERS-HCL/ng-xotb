Iconography
It is a science of identification, description, classification, and interpretation of symbols, themes, and subject matter in the visual arts. The term can also refer to the artist’s use of this imagery in a particular work.
Icons are visual symbols used to represent ideas, objects or actions. They communicate messages at a glance, afford interactivity, and draw attention to important information.
